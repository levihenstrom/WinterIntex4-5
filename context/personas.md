Explanation of why these are the most important personas: These are the most important personas because they represent the two primary ways the organization will use the application. The Program Director needs visibility into donor retention, outreach performance, organizational impact, and high-level operations so the nonprofit can stay funded and make informed strategic decisions. The Safehouse Operations Manager needs tools to manage resident cases, document interventions, monitor progress, and prevent girls from falling through the cracks across multiple safehouses. Together, these two personas cover the organization’s most urgent problems from the case: sustaining donor support and running safe, effective rehabilitation operations. While the girls are the central beneficiaries of the mission, they are not the main direct users of the system, so the product should be designed first around the staff members responsible for serving and protecting them.
