## Summary

*This transcript captures the kickoff of Intex II, a high-stakes, week-long capstone project where information systems students apply their cumulative technical skills to support a nonprofit organization. The featured client, Lighthouse Sanctuary, operates safe houses in the Philippines for young girls escaping sexual abuse and exploitation, providing them with holistic healing and a path toward hope. To respect privacy while testing student capabilities, the academic challenge requires teams to build a fully functional system for a simulated entity using synthetic data inspired by the real-world complexities of donor tracking and social media advocacy. The project demands a rigorous integration of scrum methodologies, .NET and React development, cloud deployment, and sophisticated machine learning pipelines to optimize organizational impact. Ultimately, the faculty emphasizes that this intense experience is designed to transform students into equipped professionals capable of using technology as a weapon of light against global issues.*
